Meghli

Description:

  • ½ cup Rice Flour
  • ¾ cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 tb. Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tb. Caraway
  • Toppings

Steps:

  1. Begin by combining all the dry ingredients in a medium-sized deep pot.
  2. Place the pot on the stove on medium-high heat and let come to a boil, about 5 minutes.
  3. Once the pudding has started to boil, lower the heat to low and whisk consistently for an additional 15-20 minutes until the pudding has thickened to the perfect consistency.
  4. Pour the hot pudding into 4 individual bowls.
  5. Top, Chill, and Enjoy!
